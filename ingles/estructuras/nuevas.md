1. **It’s not that…, it’s just that…**  
    No es que…, es solo que…
    

- It’s not that I don’t like the job, it’s just that the hours are too long.
    
- No es que no me guste el trabajo, es solo que las horas son demasiado largas.
    

---

2. **I can’t help + verb-ing**  
    No puedo evitar…
    

- I can’t help thinking about it.
    
- No puedo evitar pensar en ello.
    

---

3. **The thing is (that)…**  
    La cosa es que… / El problema es que…
    

- The thing is that we don’t have enough time.
    
- La cosa es que no tenemos suficiente tiempo.
    

---

4. **It’s not until… that…**  
    No es hasta… que…
    

- It’s not until you try it that you understand it.
    
- No es hasta que lo pruebas que lo entiendes.
    

---

5. **I’m not used to + verb-ing**  
    No estoy acostumbrado a…
    

- I’m not used to working at night.
    
- No estoy acostumbrado a trabajar de noche.
    

---

6. **Be supposed to + verb**  
    Se supone que… / Debería…
    

- I’m supposed to finish this today.
    
- Se supone que debo terminar esto hoy.
    

---

7. **It’s up to + someone**  
    Depende de… / Está en manos de…
    

- It’s up to you.
    
- Depende de ti.
    

---

8. **So + adjective + that…**  
    Tan… que…
    

- I was so tired that I fell asleep immediately.
    
- Estaba tan cansado que me dormí inmediatamente.
    

---

9. **Not only…, but also…**  
    No solo…, sino también…
    

- She not only speaks English, but also French.
    
- No solo habla inglés, sino también francés.
    

---

10. **By the time + clause**  
    Para cuando… / Cuando…
    

- By the time I arrived, they had already left.
    
- Para cuando llegué, ya se habían ido.

# 1. I wish + past perfect

Para arrepentimiento sobre el pasado.

**Estructura**

I wish + subject + had + past participle

**Ejemplos**

- I wish I had started earlier.
    
- I wish I had known that before.
    
- I wish I had studied more.
    

---

# 2. would rather … than …

Preferencias fuertes.

**Estructura**

would rather + verb + than + verb

**Ejemplos**

- I would rather work alone than deal with team problems.
    
- I'd rather stay home than go out tonight.
    

---

# 3. not until

Para enfatizar cuándo ocurrió algo.

**Estructura**

It wasn't until + moment + that + result

**Ejemplos**

- It wasn't until last year that I understood the problem.
    
- It wasn't until I moved abroad that I learned English.
    

---

# 4. hardly / barely … when

Algo ocurrió **justo después de otra cosa**.

**Estructura**

had hardly / barely + past participle + when

**Ejemplos**

- I had barely started working when I realized something was wrong.
    
- She had hardly arrived when the meeting started.
    

---

# 5. no matter how / what / where

Para expresar **independencia de condiciones**.

**Estructura**

No matter how + adjective/adverb + subject + verb

**Ejemplos**

- No matter how hard I try, I can't understand it.
    
- No matter what happens, I'll finish the project.
    

---

# 6. the moment / the second

Acciones **inmediatas**.

**Ejemplos**

- The moment I saw it, I knew something was wrong.
    
- The second he arrived, the meeting started.
    

---

# 7. there's no point in

Para decir que algo **no merece la pena**.

**Estructura**

there's no point in + verb-ing

**Ejemplos**

- There's no point in arguing about it.
    
- There's no point in trying again.
    

---

# 8. it's not that…, it's just that…

Para matizar una idea.

**Ejemplos**

- It's not that I don't like the job, it's just that it's exhausting.
    
- It's not that I disagree, it's just that I see it differently.
    

---

# 9. the thing is…

Para explicar algo de forma natural.

**Ejemplos**

- The thing is, I didn't expect it to be so difficult.
    
- The thing is, I don't have enough time.
    

---

# 10. it turns out that

Para revelar información.

**Ejemplos**

- It turns out that everything was a misunderstanding.
    
- It turned out that they already knew.
    

---

# 11. the reason is that

Explicación clara.

**Ejemplos**

- The reason is that we didn't have enough time.
    
- The reason is that nobody noticed the problem.
    

---

# 12. what happened was that

Muy usado en historias.

**Ejemplos**

- What happened was that I forgot the keys.
    
- What happened was that the system crashed.
