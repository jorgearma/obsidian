# 1️⃣ ¿Qué estructura es esta?

> **I would like you to tell me this.**

Es una estructura de:

### 🔹 Verb + object + to + infinitive

En este caso:

- **would like** (verbo principal)
    
- **you** (objeto)
    
- **to tell** (infinitivo con “to”)
    

---

# 2️⃣ ¿Por qué aparece “you to tell”?

Porque la estructura es:

> **Subject + would like + person + to + verb**

No es que “you” vaya con “tell”.  
“You” es el objeto del verbo “would like”.

Descomposición:

- I → sujeto
    
- would like → verbo
    
- you → objeto (¿a quién?)
    
- to tell → lo que quieres que esa persona haga
    

---

# 3️⃣ Comparación clave

### ❌ Incorrecto:

> I would like you tell me.

Siempre necesitas:

> person + to + verb

---

# 4️⃣ Ejemplos claros

### Presente / petición educada

- I would like you to help me.
    
- I would like you to be honest.
    
- I would like you to explain it again.
    
- I would like you to arrive earlier next time.
    

Traducción mental:

> Me gustaría que tú + verbo
