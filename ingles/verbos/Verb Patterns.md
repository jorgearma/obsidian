## Verb Patterns (to + verb / verb-ing)

Los **verb patterns** son reglas que indican **qué forma del verbo debe ir después de otro verbo**.  
Las dos formas principales son:

- **to + infinitive** → _to go, to eat, to study_
    
- **verb + ing** → _going, eating, studying_
    

Ejemplo:

- I **want to learn** English.
    
- I **enjoy learning** English.
    

El significado cambia si usas la forma incorrecta.

---

# 1. Verbos que siempre usan **to + verb**

Estructura:

**verb + to + base verb**

Ejemplos:

| Verb    | Ejemplo                             |
| ------- | ----------------------------------- |
| want    | I want **to learn** cybersecurity   |
| decide  | I decided **to move** to Copenhagen |
| plan    | I plan **to study** more tonight    |
| hope    | I hope **to find** a job soon       |
| need    | I need **to finish** this lab       |
| try     | I try **to understand** the system  |
| learn   | I learned **to use** Nmap           |
| agree   | He agreed **to help** me            |
| promise | I promise **to fix** it             |
| refuse  | He refused **to answer**            |

Ejemplos naturales:

- I **want to improve** my enumeration skills.
    
- I **decided to build** my own lab.
    
- I **need to practice** more English.
    

---

# 2. Verbos que siempre usan **verb-ing**

Estructura:

**verb + verb-ing**

|Verb|Ejemplo|
|---|---|
|enjoy|I enjoy **studying** English|
|avoid|I avoid **using** automatic tools first|
|finish|I finished **scanning** the network|
|keep|I keep **learning** new things|
|consider|I considered **changing** jobs|
|mind|I don't mind **working** late|
|suggest|He suggested **trying** another method|
|stop|I stopped **smoking**|

Ejemplos:

- I **enjoy solving** security problems.
    
- I **avoid using** Metasploit at the beginning.
    
- I **keep practicing** every day.
    

---

# 3. Verbos que pueden usar **las dos formas**

Algunos verbos aceptan **to + verb** o **verb-ing**.

### Sin cambio de significado

|Verb|Ejemplo|
|---|---|
|start|I started **learning / to learn** Python|
|begin|I began **working / to work** here|
|continue|I continued **studying / to study**|

---

### Con cambio de significado

Estos son **muy importantes**.

#### STOP

|Forma|Significado|
|---|---|
|stop doing|dejar de hacer algo|
|stop to do|parar para hacer algo|

Ejemplo:

- I **stopped smoking** → dejé de fumar
    
- I **stopped to smoke** → paré para fumar
    

---

#### REMEMBER

|Forma|Significado|
|---|---|
|remember doing|recuerdo haber hecho|
|remember to do|recordar hacer|

Ejemplo:

- I **remember meeting** him.
    
- Remember **to send** the email.
    

---

#### TRY

|Forma|Significado|
|---|---|
|try to do|intentar|
|try doing|probar|

Ejemplo:

- I **tried to fix** the bug.
    
- Try **restarting** the server.
    

---

#### FORGET

|Forma|Significado|
|---|---|
|forget doing|olvidar que hiciste algo|
|forget to do|olvidar hacerlo|

Ejemplo:

- I **forgot locking** the door.
    
- I **forgot to lock** the door.
    

---

# 4. Verb pattern muy común: **verb + object + to verb**

Estructura:

**verb + person + to verb**

|Ejemplo|
|---|
|I told him **to wait**|
|She asked me **to help**|
|They want us **to start**|
|The teacher told us **to repeat**|

Ejemplo real:

- The client asked us **to investigate** the breach.
    

---

# 5. Después de preposiciones → siempre **verb-ing**

Regla importante:

Después de **prepositions** usamos **-ing**

Ejemplos de preposiciones:

- after
    
- before
    
- without
    
- by
    
- for
    
- about
    
- instead of
    

Ejemplos:

- After **finishing** the scan
    
- Before **leaving** the lab
    
- Without **knowing** the password
    
- By **using** this exploit
    

Ejemplo natural:

- I improved my English **by watching** TV in English.
    

---

# 6. Expresiones comunes con **-ing**

Estas expresiones **siempre llevan -ing**.

|Expresión|Ejemplo|
|---|---|
|look forward to|I look forward to **meeting** you|
|be used to|I'm used to **working** alone|
|it's worth|It's worth **trying**|
|can't help|I can't help **laughing**|

---

# 7. Error típico (muy común en B2)

❌ I enjoy **to learn** English  
✅ I enjoy **learning** English

❌ I decided **learning** Python  
✅ I decided **to learn** Python

❌ I finished **to scan** the network  
✅ I finished **scanning** the network

---

# 8. Truco rápido para no confundirte

Piensa en tres grupos:

### Grupo A — to verb

(decisiones / planes)

- decide
    
- want
    
- plan
    
- hope
    
- promise
    
- need
    

---

### Grupo B — ing

(actividades / experiencias)

- enjoy
    
- avoid
    
- finish
    
- keep
    
- consider
    
- suggest
    

---

### Grupo C — ambos

- start
    
- begin
    
- continue
    
- try
    
- remember
    
- stop
