## 1. Opinión

|Patrón (EN)|Traducción (ES)|Ejemplo|
|---|---|---|
|I think…|Creo que…|I think technology has changed the way we communicate.|
|I believe…|Creo / considero que…|I believe people depend too much on their phones.|
|Personally…|Personalmente…|Personally, I prefer working on my own.|
|To be honest…|Para ser honesto…|To be honest, I don’t really like big cities.|
|From my point of view…|Desde mi punto de vista…|From my point of view, education is changing.|

# 2. Explicar una idea

|Patrón (EN)|Traducción (ES)|Ejemplo|
|---|---|---|
|The reason is that…|La razón es que…|The reason is that people want more freedom.|
|The thing is…|La cosa es que…|The thing is, technology moves very fast.|
|That's because…|Eso es porque…|That’s because companies compete globally.|
|This happens because…|Esto ocurre porque…|This happens because people are under pressure.|
|What I mean is…|Lo que quiero decir es que…|What I mean is that experience matters more.|

# 3. Añadir información

|Patrón (EN)|Traducción (ES)|Ejemplo|
|---|---|---|
|Also…|También…|Also, many people work remotely now.|
|In fact…|De hecho…|In fact, some companies are fully remote.|
|Actually…|En realidad…|Actually, it’s more common than people think.|
|Besides that…|Además de eso…|Besides that, it saves a lot of time.|
|On top of that…|Encima de eso / además…|On top of that, it improves productivity.|

# 4. Contrastar ideas

|Even though…|A pesar de que…|Even though people like technology, it has downsides.|
|At the same time…|Al mismo tiempo…|At the same time, it creates new opportunities.|
