# Técnica: Idea Chain

La regla es simple:

**Nunca terminas una frase.  
Siempre conectas con otra idea.**

Patrón básico:

idea → reason → contrast → result → reflection → example

---

# Paso 1 — idea inicial

Empieza con una frase simple.

Example:

> I like working as a bike courier.

---

# Paso 2 — razón

Añade **because / since**.

> I like working as a bike courier because I can organize my own schedule.

---

# Paso 3 — contraste

Añade **although / even though / but**.

> I like working as a bike courier because I can organize my own schedule, even though sometimes the weather is terrible.

---

# Paso 4 — consecuencia

Añade **so / that's why / which means**.

> I like working as a bike courier because I can organize my own schedule, even though sometimes the weather is terrible, so I just wear better clothes and keep working.

---

# Paso 5 — reflexión

Añade **actually / to be honest / in fact**.

> Actually, to be honest, freedom is more important to me than comfort.

---

# Paso 6 — ejemplo

Añade **for example / for instance**.

> For example, if I don't feel like working one day, I can simply stay home.

---

# Resultado final (fluidez natural)

> I like working as a bike courier because I can organize my own schedule, even though sometimes the weather is terrible, so I just wear better clothes and keep working. Actually, to be honest, freedom is more important to me than comfort. For example, if I don't feel like working one day, I can simply stay home.

Esto es **exactamente cómo habla alguien en B2 alto o C1**.

---

# Regla de oro del ejercicio

**No pauses.  
No pienses demasiado.  
Solo añade otra idea.**

Aunque no sea perfecto.
